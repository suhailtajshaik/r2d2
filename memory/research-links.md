### Pricing Tiers
- **Path 1 (Free):** Good for exploring, terrible for producing. Watermarks, 3-5 gens/day, 10-20 min queues
- **Path 2 ($20-50/mo):** Clean exports, unlimited iteration, fast queues, professional results. "Cheaper than Skillshare"
- **Pro tip:** Start at lowest quality (1K), upgrade winners to 4K with same prompt
- **Free Tier Survival:** One shot per concept, plan everything, crop watermarks, combine free trials, accept limitations

### Prompt Engineering for Image/Video
- Master formula: [Subject], [Action], [Environment], [Atmosphere], [Camera], [Lens], [Lighting], [Style Reference]
- "Diffusion models understand photography and cinematography, not logic"
- Cheat codes: Dutch angle, anamorphic, volumetric lighting, Kodak Portra, Cinestill 800T, chiaroscuro
- Shot framing: ECU → CU → MCU → MS → CS → MFS → FS
